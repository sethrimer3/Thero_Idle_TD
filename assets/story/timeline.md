# Project Thero — Timeline

This timeline summarizes major events before, during, and after the events of the game.

---

# Early Development (Years Before the Game)

Project Thero begins as a research collaboration at a university laboratory (Lab C137).

The team consists of:

- Slade Mercer (Mathematics)
- Tess Albright (Psychology/Linguistics)
- Matt Kline (Engineering)
- Isaac Rao (Neuroscience/Physics)
- Lydia Moreno (Biochemistry)
- Florida Sato (Medical Supervisor)

The team develops the **Mind Geiger (MG)** and the **Mind Gate interface**.

The goal is to map the structure of human cognition.

---

# Consciousness Mapping Phase

Over the course of roughly **1.5 years**, Slade volunteers as the primary test subject.

His consciousness is gradually mapped using the MG.

During this process the team discovers:

- the wavelength of consciousness
- conceptual environmental structures
- the mindscape interface

The team embeds a hidden system control architecture known as the **Master Key** within Slade’s cognitive mapping.

---

# Prologue

Final testing of the Mind Gate begins.

During the first full immersion test, the funding organization steals the machine.

Slade’s consciousness remains inside the mindscape while the MG is transported away.

Communication with the lab is lost.

---

# Chapter 1

Slade explores the mindscape alone.

He begins understanding how mathematical structures stabilize the environment.

He realizes the machine must have been taken from the lab.

---

# Chapter 2

The professors re-establish partial communication using salvaged equipment.

They confirm that the Mind Gate was stolen.

Slade cannot yet be extracted from the system.

---

# Chapter 3

The team develops a method to extract Slade.

During synchronization they discover the stolen MG is broadcasting cognitive influence signals.

Slade chooses to remain inside the mindscape to counteract the influence.

---

# Chapter 4

The team realizes multiple machines are broadcasting archetypal signals.

A global **cognitive war** begins.

Slade stabilizes distortions inside the collective unconscious.

---

# Chapter 5

Researchers using the stolen technology detect interference.

They begin searching for the **Master Key holder**.

The professors become fugitives.

Before communication is lost, Tess enters the mindscape to help Slade.

---

# Chapter 6

Slade and Tess defend the mindscape against hostile agents.

The professors anonymously release research about cognitive manipulation.

Global awareness begins weakening the broadcasts.

---

# The Long Vigil (Decades)

Slade and Tess remain inside the mindscape for decades.

They reshape archetypal patterns toward healthier structures.

Human societies gradually become more resistant to manipulation.

---

# Final Retrieval

The surviving professors rebuild the Mind Gate safely.

Slade and Tess are retrieved from the mindscape.

They awaken in a world shaped by decades of increased cognitive awareness.

The peace they helped create feels unfamiliar to them.
