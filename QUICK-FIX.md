## The Problem
You merged changes to `main`, but they're not visible when you play the game.

## The Solution
**Your browser cached the old files. Force it to re-download:**

### Windows/Linux
Press: **`Ctrl + Shift + R`** or **`Ctrl + F5`**

### Mac
Press: **`Cmd + Shift + R`**

## Still Not Working?

### Clear Site Data (Chrome/Edge/Brave)
1. Press `F12` to open DevTools
2. Right-click the refresh button â†»
3. Click **"Empty Cache and Hard Reload"**

### Clear Everything (Nuclear Option)
1. Press `F12` to open DevTools
2. Go to **Application** tab
3. Click **"Clear storage"** in sidebar
4. Click **"Clear site data"** button
5. Refresh the page
